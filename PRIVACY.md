# Privacy Policy

**MorseStudio** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data (conversion history) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is sent to any server. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.

**Contact:** Open an issue at https://github.com/divijsahu/Morse-Code/issues
